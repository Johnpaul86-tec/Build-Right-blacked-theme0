What We Do

Material Supply

At BuildRight, we understand that strong projects begin with reliable materials. That’s why we supply high-quality cement, steel, and roofing essentials to builders, contractors, and institutions across Kenya. Each product is sourced from trusted manufacturers to guarantee strength, durability, and performance. Our goal is to simplify construction by ensuring clients have what they need, when they need it. With BuildRight, you’re not just buying materials—you’re investing in reliability, consistency, and long-lasting results.

Construction

Construction is at the core of BuildRight’s expertise. From residential houses to commercial complexes, we deliver projects that combine safety, durability, and modern design.
